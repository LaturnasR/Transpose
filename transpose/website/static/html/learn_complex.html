1. Read

Read the following problem: The difference of x and five times a number.

First, we look for words indicating an unknown quantity. We have 'x', which was already assigned a variable, and 'a number' which is yet to be assigned. Since 'x' was already used to represent an unknown quantity, we should use another letter to assign 'a number'. Usually, we just use the next letter 'y', this will make our phrase to be: difference of x and five times y.

2. Identify

Next, we have to identify the keywords in our phrase. In our case, we have two operator keywords namely: 'the difference of' and 'times' (highlighted in green). Each operator keywords have their own operands (highlighted in blue).
Since this phrase is ambiguous, it could have more than one possible answers depending on how it is interpreted.

Interpretation 1

The keyword 'times' has 'the difference of x and 5' and 'y' as its operands:

The difference of x and 5 times y

Since 'the difference of x and 5' is another operation, it has its own operator and operands as well:

The difference of x and 5



Interpretation 2:

The keyword 'the difference of' has 'x' and '5 times y' as its operands:

The difference of x and 5 times y

Since '5 times y' is another operation, it has its own operator and operands as well:

5 times y

3. Analyze

Now it is time to apply the meaning of our identified operator keywords.

Interpretation 1

Let's start with the keyword 'times', as it has the complete phrase with its operands. Times simply means to multiply, which means that we will multiply the first operand 'the difference of x and 5' by the second operand 'y'. Since 'the difference of x and 5' is another operation within an operation, it is to be performed first. The keyword 'the difference of' means that the first operand 'x' will be subtracted by the second operand '5'


Interpretation 2

Let's start with the keyword 'the difference of', as it has the complete phrase with its operands. It means that the first operand 'x' will be subtracted by the second operand '5 times y'. Since '5 times y' is another operation within an operation, it is to be performed first. The keyword 'times' simply means that we multiply the first operand '5' by the second operand 'y'.

4. Convert

Lastly, we just have to convert the idea into mathematical symbols.

The difference of x and 5 → `x - 5`


multiply the result by y → `(x - 5) * y)`
