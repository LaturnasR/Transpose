 What is Transpose


  Transpose is a website that acts as a supplemental tool for students, especially Grade 7 students, in learning a specific topic namely: "Translating English phrases to mathematical expressions". The tool does exactly what the topic says, it translates english phrases inputted by the user, then outputs its mathematical expression equivalent.


  Transpose aims to lessen the confusion students face when translating worded problems, like operator precedence and the difference between the usage of keywords and their impact on the problem's translated outcome. Transpose also provides short guides to how these answers are formulated, with brief instructions that students can follow and apply to future problems without relying on the calculator.


 Why Transpose


  According to Programme for International Student Assessment or PISA's statistics in 2018, the mean score of 15 year-old Filipinos' performance in secondary level mathematics is 353, that is 136 points lower than the average of 489 points among 77 countries participating in PISA, making the Philippines ranked 75th or the 3rd lowest performing country. According to the studies, one of the students' hindrance in learning mathematics is comprehending word problems. Students are having difficulties formulating these problems to turn into math expressions for easier solving.
